# Medical Cybersecurity Research

- **Status:** In Progress
- **Date:** 2026-06-10
- **Category:** Healthcare / Security

## Purpose

Collect early observations about **Medical Cybersecurity**, especially clinical data input, validation, privacy, and workflow risk.

## Setup

- Research notes
- Healthcare workflow observations
- Security risk checklist
- Prototype ideas for validation systems

## Steps

1. Identify manual data entry points.
2. List possible validation failures.
3. Map basic security and privacy concerns.
4. Connect observations to possible prototype features.

## Result

The research suggests that data validation and workflow design are both important. Security cannot be separated from how people actually use a system.

## Notes

This is research-oriented and should stay careful. The goal is to learn and prototype, not to claim clinical readiness.

## Next Step

Turn selected risks into small testable prototype requirements.
