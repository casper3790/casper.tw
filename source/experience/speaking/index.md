# Speaking & Training

## Overview

This page records teaching and training support roles. I avoid labeling these as formal conference speaking unless that is accurate. Most of this work is hands-on support for students and workshops.

## Cards

### CTF Instructor

Supported learning around Web Security, Linux basics, OSINT, and CTF practice workflows.

### Raspberry Pi Instructor

Helped learners work with **Raspberry Pi**, Linux, GPIO concepts, Python, and simple IoT experiments.

### Cybersecurity Teaching Assistant

Assisted Cybersecurity learning activities, helped with practice flow, and supported students during exercises.

### Workshop Support

Helped prepare materials, guide participants, and keep the workshop process moving.

### Training Support

Supported technical training sessions by helping participants debug setup and understand task steps.

Teaching support helps me find gaps in my own understanding. If I cannot explain a concept clearly, I need to learn it again.
