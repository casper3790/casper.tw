# Community & Teaching

## Overview

Community work is part of how I learn. It gives technical learning a place to continue after a class, competition, or workshop ends.

## Timeline

- **NIHSCEDIWHC White Hat Community** - Cybersecurity community building and knowledge sharing.
- **Cybersecurity community building** - Helped create spaces for students to discuss security topics and practice together.
- **Cybersecurity Teaching Assistant** - Supported practice sessions and helped students with technical questions.
- **Student Representative** - Helped coordinate communication and student activities.
- **CTF training** - Supported practice around Web Security, Linux, OSINT, and challenge solving.
- **Raspberry Pi teaching** - Helped students learn Linux, GPIO, Python, and embedded experiments.

## Notes

I try to keep community work practical. The goal is not only to say "learn Cybersecurity", but to create repeatable practice paths.
