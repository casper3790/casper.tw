# Competitions

## Overview

This page records competitions that shaped my learning across **Cybersecurity**, **Robotics**, programming, and AI. I treat competitions as practical deadlines: they force me to build, present, debug, and learn from constraints.

## Timeline

- **InnoServe** - Project-based competition experience and finalist preparation.
- **Cybersecurity Hackathon** - OSINT, Web Security, Linux, and CTF-oriented practice under time pressure.
- **Robotics competitions** - Mechanical, electrical, control, and teamwork practice.
- **FTC** - Robotics team experience with engineering design, testing, and iteration.
- **Programming / AI competitions** - Algorithmic thinking, data handling, and prototype delivery.
- **Competition finalists** - Experience preparing demos, documentation, and presentation materials.

## Notes

I do not see competitions only as ranking results. They are checkpoints for checking whether a system is understandable, testable, and useful under pressure.
