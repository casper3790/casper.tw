## Who am I?

My name is **Casper**.

I am a student builder from Taiwan, mainly focusing on **Cybersecurity**, **CTF**, **Web Development**, and **Robotics**.

Most of the time, I learn by building, testing, breaking, fixing, and writing things down.  
From CTF practice to websites, from technical notes to project development, I am still exploring what kind of person I want to become — and this blog is part of that journey.

I am especially interested in:

- Web Security
- CTF Problem Solving
- Technical Writing
- Robotics / Engineering Projects
- AI × Security × Daily-Life Applications
