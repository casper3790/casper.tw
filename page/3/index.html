Why Casper Exists

Before the First Page

If you are reading this,

maybe you are also looking for a direction worth following.

I am not the best test taker, and I am not the strongest engineer.

I simply stepped into technology because of curiosity.

Later, I learned that what changed me was not one tool or one skill.

It was the habit of exploring, building, and sharing what I understood along the way.

So Casper is not a resume.

It is more like a research notebook that is still being written.

I hope one day, when someone else feels unsure,

this place can help them start their first project.

If that day comes,

that is why Casper exists.
